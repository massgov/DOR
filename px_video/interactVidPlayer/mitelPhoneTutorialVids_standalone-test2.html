MiCollab Videos and Resources

MiCollab is a communications tool that allows users from Tax and CSE to make and receive phone calls from their telework computer or smartphone. Individuals in Tax and CSE will receive an email with an authentication key. This email will come from “noreply@mitel.easydeploy.net”. To learn more about setting up your MiCollab softphone, Tax and CSE individuals should see:

  • MiCollab Desktop User Guide
  • Micollab Cheat Sheet

Please note: MiCollab is currently only available to Tax and CSE users.
